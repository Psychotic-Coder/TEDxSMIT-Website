tedxSMIT 2015
Freedom is Power

Freedom is power, but requires courage to unveil its strength. Courage is strength, but requires freedom to explore its power. TEDxSMIT will celebrate the ideas where freedom met courage to spark actions that have the potential to impact the civilization. It will give the stage to people who can inspire and engage through their stories and initiate the movement of unveiling and exploring.
